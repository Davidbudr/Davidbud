Time Me Card Art

Download 
With all of the Cards in the Scrapbook sections, I decided that I would go for a colourless style to match the first card that I made for the scrapbook which was the mirror me prototype card, with that card I simply removed the colours from the finished versions card art because I wanted to show that this was an incomplete version of the game, however, I didn't finish the project so I did the process the correct way around.

My idea for Time mes' art was that it would show the repeating nature of the game, for this I went with a mandala/radial pattern to make it look like the player is chasing themselves.

To make it look a little more mystical I wanted to add some writing around the edges so I decided to go with Tolkeins Elvish saying Dark Times, repeatedly, this being a nod to the player's recorded copy being a shadow.
